The Summit

Smart Education Summit 2024 is a strategic platform aimed at discovering inventive approaches to tackle challenges in the K-12 school education sector, using design thinking, innovative solutions, or region-specific technology in the MENA region. This exclusive platform is the sixteenth event in CEI Group’s Smart Summit Series, following successful editions such as STEM Education & Innovation Summit, Smart Teaching & Experiential Learning Summit, Smart Teaching & Experiential Learning – Online Summit & Smart Education Online Summit.

International solution providers: The summit will help to establish productive collaborations between international solution providers and regional school administrators.

Smart

In the context of Smart Education Summit – means creative, innovative ways of addressing and overcoming the challenges in the education ecosystem, may it be with the help of design thinking, innovative solutions or technology specific to the MENA region.

UAE VISION

The UAE vision National Agenda emphasizes the development of a first-rate education system, which will require a complete transformation of the current education system and teaching methods. The National Agenda aims for all schools, universities and students to be equipped with Smart systems and devices as a basis for all teaching methods, projects and research.
